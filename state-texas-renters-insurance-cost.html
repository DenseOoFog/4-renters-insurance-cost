Texas Renters Insurance Cost Guide (2026)

Many renters in Texas typically see monthly premiums around $16-$33. Your final quote depends on city-level risk, prior claims, building characteristics, and policy settings.

Texas Cost Snapshot

MetricTypical Range

Key Cost Drivers in Texas

  • City-specific weather and water-risk differences.
  • Property security features and neighborhood claim patterns.
  • Deductible and liability limit decisions.
  • Discount eligibility (bundle, security, autopay).

Coverage Suggestions for Texas Renters

  • Set property limit based on documented item inventory.
  • Use a deductible you can handle without cashflow stress.
  • Compare at least three quotes with same settings before choosing.
