Illinois Renters Insurance Cost Guide (2026)

Illinois renters often pay around $14-$29/month. Your quote usually changes based on location, building characteristics, policy limits, prior claims, and deductible choices.

Illinois Cost Snapshot

MetricTypical Range
Monthly premium$14-$29
Common deductible$500
Property coverage$20,000-$40,000
Liability limit$100,000-$300,000

Key Cost Drivers in Illinois

  • Neighborhood claim patterns and security setup.
  • Apartment building age and maintenance quality.
  • Coverage limit choices for electronics and valuables.
  • Discount eligibility (bundle, autopay, alarms).

Coverage Guidance

  • Create a simple item inventory before choosing property limits.
  • Compare three quotes with the same deductible settings.
  • Review policy terms at each lease renewal.
